  • 9型人格类型
  • 1. Reformer
  • 2. Helper
  • 3. Achiever
  • 4. Individualist
  • 5. Investigator
  • 6. Loyalist
  • 7. Enthusiast
  • 8. Challenger
  • 9. Peacemaker

1. Reformer

  • idealist and principled，self-convinced and justice-oriented，
    raise thing up to their highest version；

  • 理想主义者和原则主义者，自信且以正义为导向，将事物提升到最高境界；

2. Helper

  • in love of the love，the one who love to please and
    irradiate kindness by delivering themself to other；

  • 热爱爱情，喜欢取悦他人并通过向他人奉献自己来散发善意的人；

3. Achiever

  • energic type with lot of self-awareness that could turn
    into someone empowered，pure potential and determination；

  • 精力充沛的人，具有很强的自我意识，可成为被赋予权力的人，具有纯粹的潜力和决心；

4. Individualist

  • eternal walker of inward hall，who can create masterpiece
    and has to trespass inner current to reach natural self；

  • 内心殿堂的永恒行者，能创造杰作，必须突破内心潮流才能达到自然自我；

5. Investigator

  • investigator of golden source，tenacious explorer of
    human mind grab in his hand secretive brilliance；

  • 黄金源头的调查者，人类心灵的顽强探索者，手中握有秘密的光辉；

6. Loyalist

  • tenacious guardians of their value，will fight for safety
    and consistency，friendly and earnest，embody contradiction；

  • 顽强的价值观守护者，将为安全和一致性而战，友好而认真，体现矛盾；

7. Enthusiast

  • multitasker and cheerful，

  • 热情、多面手和开朗，认为自己去某个地方要迟到，或错过一些重要的事情；

8. Challenger

  • rampaging spirit want to eat world，powerful
    and self-confident and always on defense；

  • 想要吞噬世界的狂暴精神，强大而自信，始终处于防御状态；

9. Peacemaker

  • reassuring one try to keep peace of mind and
    serenity whether inside and outside of them；

  • 令人安心的人，无论在内心还是外在，都试图保持内心的平静和宁静；
